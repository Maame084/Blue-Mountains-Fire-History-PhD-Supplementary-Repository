# CHAR and CharAnalysis Outputs

This folder contains processed charcoal accumulation datasets, CHARa and CHARno calculations, CharAnalysis outputs, charcoal transformations, fire-episode detection outputs, screening analyses, interpolated charcoal series, and supplementary analytical products associated with the reconstruction of fire regime variability in the Upper Blue Mountains, eastern Australia.

The datasets support interpretations of temporal variability in charcoal accumulation, fire frequency, significant fire years, and broader landscape-scale fire activity across the twentieth century.

Contents include:

* CHARa and CHARno datasets
* CharAnalysis outputs
* Peak detection and screening analyses
* Charcoal transformations and interpolated series
* Composite charcoal datasets
* Fire-return interval summaries
* Supplementary figures and analytical outputs
